Estas son las extensiones del Firefox que tengo instaladas y que me resultan imprescindibles:

[Aardvark](http://karmatics.com/aardvark/ "Quickly clean up a page prior to printing. Also web developer tools.") 1.1  
[Bloglines Toolkit](http://jayseae.cxliv.org/ "Browser utilities for use with Bloglines") 1.6.2  
[CSSViewer](http://www.google.com/search?&q=CSSViewer "Search Google for CSSViewer") 1.0.1  
[CuteMenus 2](http://www.geocities.com/replysn/firefox.htm "Iconifies your menus and popups with XP skin.") 1.0  
[del.icio.us](http://del.icio.us/ "Keep, share and discover all your favorite things.") 1.1  
[Disable Targets For Downloads](http://www.cusser.net "Prevents download links opening a blank window.") 1.0.1  
[Download Manager Tweak](http://dmextension.mozdev.org/ "A modification of the Firefox download manager that changes its appearance and allows it to be opened in a separate window, a new tab, or the sidebar.") 0.7.1  
[Feedview](http://www.epigoon.com "Make your news feeds nicer to read") 0.9.8  
[Gmail Notifier](http://www.nexgenmedia.net/extensions/ "A notifier for Gmail accounts.") 0.5.5.5  
[IE View](http://ieview.mozdev.org/ "Open pages in IE via Firefox menus") 1.2.7  
[ListZilla](http://roachfiend.com "Outputs an alphabetical list of extensions to a text, vB code, or HTML file.") 0.7  
[Live HTTP Headers](http://livehttpheaders.mozdev.org/ "View HTTP headers of a page and while browsing.") 0.11  
[PDF Download](http://www.rabotat.org/firefox/ "Allows to choose what you want to do with a PDF file: download it, view it with an external viewer or view it as HTML!") 0.6  
[SearchPluginHacks](http://www.iosart.com/firefox/searchpluginhacks/ "Allows uninstalling search plugins - just right click on a search plugin and choose 'Delete'") 0.1.3  
[Session Manager](http://sessionmanager.mozdev.org/ "Saves and restores the state of all windows.") 0.4.1.1  
[Tweak Network](http://www.bitstorm.org/extensions/tweak/ "Tweak network settings.") 1.1  
[Web Developer](http://chrispederick.com/work/webdeveloper/ "Adds a menu and a toolbar with various web developer tools.") 1.0.2  

Esta es una de esas entradas _'pospami'_, así, la próxima vez que tenga que reinstalar todo no tendre que mirar en el portatil cuales tengo instaladas...
